Competitive Gamemodes

This is a wider card with supporting text Looking to be a star player in your group? Here are some tips for how to help your team!

Remember to check out your teams compisition. Sometimes Lúcio just wont work with a team and that's okay! But in most teams he can be a valuable ally with his Speed Boost.

Another helpful reminder, instead of wallriding, try wall Hopping! It can be faster than wallriding but in times where your team needs heals or speed and you want to be safe? Wallglide near them or crouch and strafe!

Stadium Builds

Stadium, is an incredible gamemode for fun and a good way to build around your team instead of having to swap around to find out what works! Although, in Stadium once you pick a hero you cannot swap them anyways.

In stadium, it's important to take into account once again, your team and try to build around them but at the same time? Build to how you plan to use him.

Remember to read carefully what everything does! As you could be using the wrong thing without knowing it! For example Lucios healing goes off of his Ability power whilst Mercy and Juno's healing goes off of their weapon power.

Looking to improve?

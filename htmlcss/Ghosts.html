Ghosts

Some knowledge we have on Ghosts around the area.

Be safe rather than sorry, know about these guys and avoid them if possible.

SLIMER

A class 5 full roaming ghost with an appetite. Wherever he goes, he will leave a trail of slime behind him.

JOGGER GHOST

Spirit of a man who once jogged in Central Park and now continues to jog even after death and pushes people out of his path.

MUNCHER

A free-floating metal eating ghost, a class 5 that prefers metallic objects as a meal but can reform them into destructive pellets.

STAY-PUFT MARSHMALLOW MAN

Hilarious as this sounds, he is a dangerous ghost! There are miniature versions of him that cause havoc wherever they go (mini-puffs).
